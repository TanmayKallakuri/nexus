================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
================================================================

1. LLM is the dominant factor. Adds +0.24 to +0.26 correlation
   to any ML model. Contributes ~85% of final signal.

2. ML model choice matters less. Tolendi vs Ours = 0.03 diff
   in correlation. Both are adequate ML backbones.

3. Higher LLM weight improves accuracy but slightly hurts
   correlation. 0.3/0.7 optimizes for correlation, 0.1/0.9
   optimizes for accuracy.

4. News Sharing is the easiest category. All models score
   Strong, even ML-only.

5. Political & Social Views benefits most from LLM.
   ML-only: r=0.097-0.135. With LLM: r=0.524-0.541.
   The LLM reads political affiliation from persona text.

6. LLM model used: Claude Sonnet (claude-sonnet-4-20250514)
   Temperature: 0.3, few-shot with 3 training examples.
